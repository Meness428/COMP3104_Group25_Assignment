Information about George Brown College.
George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto that offers a variety of Certificate, Diploma, Advanced Diploma, Bachelor and Post Graduate programs.
This allows students to choose a field they prefer and can start their post secondary education or further it by specializing in their field.
The program to highlight is the Computer Programming and Analysis Advanced Diploma program. This program offers students the option to complete 
their studies fully online or hybrid (online and on campus). The online option is only available for Domestic students. This exhibits that George 
Brown offers programs with some flexibility enabling students outside of the GTA to complete their studies from home.
