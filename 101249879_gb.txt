Information about George Brown College.
George Brown College is a public college, located in Toronto, that offers a variety of Certificate, 
Diploma, Advanced Diploma, Bachelor and Post Graduate programs. Allowing students to choose a field 
they prefer and can start their post secondary education or further it by specializing in their field. 
The program to highlight is the Computer Programming and Analysis Advanced Diploma program. This program 
offers students the option to complete their studies fully online or hybrid (online and on campus). The 
online option is only available for Domestic students. This exhibits the flexibility some of George Brown 
College's programs offer enabling students outside of the GTA to complete their studies from home.
