DevOps is crutial to Software Development Life Cycle (SLDC) as it allows for a much more efficient and faster software delivery method.
It allows for better teamwork by making it easier to collaborate with other teammates and also improves communication and reduces delays by speeding up the entire process.
DevOps is also streamlines workflows by minimizing downtime which can cut costs while improving overall productivity.
DevOps encourages ongoing monitoring and feedback, helping teams spot issues in real time. 
This means problems can be fixed faster, leading to better quality software over time.
